About Me

I am currently a student at Eleven Fifty Academy studying to become a junior web developer. After nearly 8 years in the healthcare industry as a medical biller and feeling like a number instead of a person, I decided it was time for a change. I started researching my options and taking personality and aptitude tests to try and find what new path would make me feel challenged and fulfilled. While discussing this with my husband he mentioned a friend of his who had graduated from Eleven Fifty Academy in early 2017 and mentioned it my be something I like. The research started and 3 months later I was enrolled and starting my new path.

My first week was tough, but all it did was solidify in my mind that I had made the right choice. After class I would go home, feed and play with my little girl until her bed time, and then immerse myself in coding. Hours could pass that I wouldn't even notice until my husband told me to go to bed.
